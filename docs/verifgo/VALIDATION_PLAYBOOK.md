# VerifGo QC Validation Playbook

## Gate Before Heavy Build

Interview 15 Quebec drivers.

The gate passes only if:

- 6 drivers are willing to pay.
- 3 drivers are willing to install a test version.
- 1 driver agrees to next-day real use.

If this gate fails, do not continue into full MVP build. Fix positioning first.

## Interview Script

1. How long have you driven Uber, rideshare, or taxi in Quebec?
2. Do you know about the daily vehicle verification requirement?
3. How do you record it today?
4. Have you or someone you know ever had trouble showing the report?
5. If an app reminded you every morning and let you complete the report in about 30 seconds, would you use it?
6. Would you pay around $5/month or $29/year?
7. Would you install a test version this week?
8. Would you try it tomorrow morning before the first ride?

## Pilot Gate

After MVP prototype:

- 10 drivers installed.
- 5 drivers use it for at least 5 days.
- At least 3 drivers say they would keep it installed.
