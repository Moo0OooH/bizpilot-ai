## 1. Product Identity

BizPilot AI is a universal AI-powered intake and lead-conversion platform for local service businesses.

The commercial entry point is narrow and focused:

**Cleaning-first go-to-market with an editable Cleaning Smart Quote Template.**

The product core is intentionally broader:

**Universal Smart Intake + AI Lead Conversion Core.**

This means BizPilot AI is not only a cleaning form, and it is not a full CRM. It is the layer before CRM: the system that helps a local business capture better requests, understand lead quality, prepare faster replies, and follow up consistently.

---

## 2. Final Strategic Formula

The canonical strategy is:

**Product Core = Universal Smart Intake + AI Lead Conversion**  
**Go-To-Market = Cleaning-first**  
**MVP Template = Editable Cleaning Smart Quote Template**  
**Expansion Model = Industry Templates after Validation Gate**

This strategy protects two priorities at the same time:

1. The MVP remains focused enough to build and sell.
2. The platform foundation remains reusable for future verticals.

## 3. One-Line Definition

BizPilot AI gives local service businesses a branded smart intake page that turns customer requests into structured leads, missing-info insights, lead quality signals, AI reply drafts, follow-up drafts, and simple daily action guidance.

## 4. Customer-Facing Positioning

For early cleaning customers, BizPilot AI should be explained simply:

**Stop losing quote requests. Reply faster. Follow up better. Book more jobs.**

Secondary sales message:

**Recover quote requests that normally get lost in DMs, texts, and delayed replies.**

Trust message:

**BizPilot does not message your customers automatically. It prepares drafts. You stay in control.**

More detailed message:

**BizPilot gives your cleaning business a branded smart quote page that collects the right information, prepares professional reply drafts, detects missing details, and helps you follow up without needing a full CRM.**

### 4.1 Commercial Offer

BizPilot should be sold as an outcome-first system, not as AI software.

Primary offer:

**Get a branded quote page that helps you reply faster, follow up better, and book more cleaning jobs.**

Do not lead with:

- AI platform
- Intake automation
- Lead conversion engine
- CRM alternative

Lead with:

- Fewer missed quote requests
- Faster replies
- More complete customer details
- Easier follow-up
- More booked jobs

### 4.2 Public Link Distribution Strategy

Each business must receive a simple public intake link that can be placed anywhere the business already receives customer attention.

MVP distribution targets:

- Business website button or link
- Instagram bio
- Facebook page
- Google Business Profile website/booking link area where appropriate
- Email signature
- SMS/DM manual reply
- QR code on flyers, vehicles, or business cards later

MVP does not require a full embeddable widget or iframe. A clean hosted branded page is safer, faster, easier to support, and enough for validation.

Future distribution options:

- Lightweight website embed
- Custom domain
- QR generator
- UTM/source tracking
- Multiple campaign-specific intake links

## 7. Core Customer Problem

Local service businesses lose customers because the lead workflow is messy.

Common problems:

- Customer messages are incomplete.
- Requests arrive through Instagram, Facebook, WhatsApp, Google Business Profile, SMS, phone, or website forms.
- Business owners repeat the same questions manually.
- Replies are delayed.
- Follow-ups are forgotten.
- Owners do not know which lead is most urgent or complete.
- Leads are not organized into a simple workflow.
- Small businesses are not ready for a full CRM.

The core problem is not only lead capture.

The real problem is:

**Turning messy customer interest into a clear next action that can become a booked job.**

## 9. Product Principles

### 9.1 Universal Core, Narrow Entry

The core system must be reusable across multiple service categories, but sales and marketing must start with one niche: cleaning.

### 9.2 Editable Templates, Not Fixed Forms

Industry Packs are editable templates, not hardcoded forms.

The Cleaning Smart Quote Template gives a strong starting structure, but the business or concierge setup flow can adjust labels, required fields, field order, and success messaging.

### 9.3 Lead Conversion, Not Form Submission

The product must not stop at collecting form data.

It must convert a submission into:

- Lead summary
- Missing information
- Lead quality score
- Suggested next action
- Reply draft
- Follow-up draft
- Owner action item

### 9.4 AI Assistant, Not AI Operator

AI helps the owner. It does not replace the owner.

The owner reviews, edits, copies, and sends messages manually during MVP.

### 9.5 Concierge-First Early Sales

The first customers should not be forced into a complex self-serve setup.

MoOoH can set up the business manually and sell a done-for-you service-first MVP.

### 9.6 Privacy-First

The product must collect only useful data, protect tenant data, and clearly explain consent on public intake pages.

### 9.7 Validation Before Expansion

No second vertical, booking system, growth studio, or integration should be built until the validation gate is passed.

## 12. Universal Smart Intake Core

The Universal Smart Intake Core is the central product foundation.

It should support:

- Dynamic intake forms
- Configurable fields
- Public branded page rendering
- Form submissions
- Conversion of submission into lead
- Industry templates
- Template customization
- AI analysis after submission

The Universal Smart Intake Core must also support controlled form-to-output mapping. This means custom fields are not only collected; they can influence the lead summary, missing-info detection, reply draft, follow-up draft, and suggested next action.

For MVP, this must stay controlled through template settings, field metadata, FAQ, service catalog, and response preferences. It must not become arbitrary prompt editing for customers.

The MVP must not become a full drag-and-drop form builder.

MVP supports simple configuration only:

- Field label
- Field type
- Required or optional
- Field order
- Help text
- Placeholder text
- Active/inactive state
- Success message
- Consent notice

## 14. Admin Custom Field and Output Control

BizPilot must support controlled admin/concierge configuration for custom fields and desired lead outputs.

MVP capabilities:

- Admin/concierge can add simple custom fields to an intake form.
- Admin/concierge can set field labels, required status, order, help text, and visibility.
- Admin/concierge can mark fields as important for lead quality or missing-info detection.
- Admin/concierge can define the business output preference, such as “short reply,” “professional quote request reply,” or “ask missing questions first.”
- AI outputs must use the active template, service catalog, FAQ, privacy mode, and field metadata.

MVP restrictions:

- No arbitrary customer-written AI prompts.
- No full workflow builder.
- No drag-and-drop builder.
- No advanced conditional logic.
- No automatic outbound messaging.

This gives flexibility without making the MVP too complex.

## 20. Lead Quality Score

Lead Quality Score helps the owner prioritize.

MVP score categories:

- Strong
- Good
- Needs Info
- Low Fit

Rule-based starting criteria:

- Strong = contact + service + area + preferred timing + required fields complete.
- Good = contact + service + area complete, but some useful optional information is missing.
- Needs Info = one or more key quote details are missing.
- Low Fit = outside service area, unclear request, or poor match with offered services.

The score starts rule-based in MVP and may become AI-assisted after the core workflow works.

Example inputs:

- Contact information present
- Service type selected
- Preferred date provided
- Location/service area provided
- Important required fields complete
- Notes quality
- Match with business service area

AI generates:

- Lead summary
- Reply draft
- Follow-up draft

- Tone variants

## 24. Privacy and Consent

Privacy is part of the product.

### 23.1 MVP Privacy Modes

MVP supports two privacy modes:

1. Standard Mode
2. Minimal Data Mode

### 23.2 Future Privacy Mode

Forward-Only Mode is future only.

It is excluded from MVP because it adds retention, deletion, support visibility, and forwarding complexity.

### 23.3 Standard Mode

Stores:

- Submission data
- Lead data
- AI outputs
- Lead status
- Basic usage events

### 23.4 Minimal Data Mode

Stores only useful minimum data, such as:

- Business ID
- Customer name
- Customer contact
- Service type
- City/service area
- Status
- Created date
- Minimal notes where appropriate

### 23.5 Canonical Consent Notice

The public intake page must display:

**By submitting this request, you agree that your information will be shared with [Business Name] to respond to your quote request. BizPilot may help prepare internal AI drafts, but the business reviews messages before sending.**

## 28. Validation Gate

Before building Booking Lite, second verticals, Growth Studio, integrations, or advanced automation, the following must be true:

- 3 businesses onboarded
- 30 real or semi-real leads submitted
- 50% owner review rate
- 30% AI draft copy/edit usage
- 1 paying or payment-ready customer

If the gate is not passed, do not build new features.

Investigate:

- Positioning
- Demo quality
- Onboarding friction
- Pricing
- Customer segment
- Form friction
- AI output quality
- Owner workflow
