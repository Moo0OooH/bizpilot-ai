# BizPilot AI - Founder CRM and Pilot Tracking Workflow v1.0

## Purpose

Define how the founder tracks prospects, demos, pilots, objections, and conversion readiness before building a full CRM.

## Tooling

Primary tool for Phase 18:

```text
artifacts/phase18/BizPilot_Phase18_Founder_CRM_Template.xlsx
```

The `/admin` console may show operational signals, but the founder CRM remains the source of outreach truth until validation.

## Required Fields

```text
business name
owner name
phone/email
city
lead source
demo status
pilot status
plan
payment readiness
quote link placements
lead count
AI draft usage
owner review rate
objections
next follow-up date
notes
```

## Stages

```text
prospect
contacted
demo scheduled
demo completed
pilot setup
active pilot
payment-ready
paid
paused
lost
```

## Weekly Founder Loop

Every week:

```text
add new cleaning prospects
contact at least 5 qualified owners
record exact owner wording
record objections
log demo result
check quote link placements
check real lead count
check AI draft copy/edit evidence
schedule next follow-up
```

## Validation Signals

BizPilot cannot expand beyond cleaning quote recovery until:

```text
3 paying or payment-ready cleaning businesses
repeat weekly usage
real quote submissions
AI drafts save owner time
at least one strong owner value statement
healthy retention signal after onboarding
```
