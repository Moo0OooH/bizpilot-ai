# BizPilot AI — Pilot Onboarding & Founder CRM Workflow v1.0

## Purpose
Define how the founder should manually onboard and track early cleaning business pilots.

## Target
First 3 paying or payment-ready cleaning businesses.

## Founder CRM Fields
- company name
- owner name
- phone/email
- city
- website/social links
- lead source
- current quote channels
- estimated monthly quote requests
- pain level
- demo status
- onboarding status
- pilot start date
- plan
- payment readiness
- quote link placements
- weekly usage notes
- objections
- next action
- follow-up date

## Onboarding Steps
1. Discovery call or DM.
2. Confirm current quote flow.
3. Create business account.
4. Configure business profile.
5. Configure quote page.
6. Create test lead.
7. Show Lead Recovery Desk.
8. Show AI summary/reply draft.
9. Confirm owner understands no auto-send.
10. Place quote link in at least two places.
11. Schedule 7-day check-in.

## Week 1 Success Signals
- owner opens dashboard
- at least one lead captured
- owner reviews AI draft
- owner copies or edits a draft
- missing info guidance is useful
- owner says it saves time or improves response consistency

## Expansion Gate
Do not expand verticals or automate channels until validation metrics are met.
