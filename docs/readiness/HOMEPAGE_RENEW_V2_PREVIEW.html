FAQ

Common questions from cleaning owners.

Will BizPilot send messages to my customers automatically?
No. Never. BizPilot only prepares drafts inside your dashboard. You read, edit, then copy and send through your own Instagram, email, or SMS. Nothing leaves your account without your manual action.
How is this different from Jobber or Housecall Pro?
Jobber and Housecall Pro manage booked jobs — scheduling, dispatch, invoicing. BizPilot helps you win the job in the first place, by replying faster to messy DM quote requests. You can use both: BizPilot up front, the others after the booking.
Do I need to integrate Instagram, WhatsApp, or SMS?
No. You share one quote link in your Instagram bio, Google Business profile, or saved text reply. The customer fills it out. The lead lands in your dashboard. No API integrations.
How is customer privacy handled?
Each business has tenant-isolated data. The AI never sees more than the minimum needed for a draft. Raw customer messages are never logged. Designed with Quebec Law 25 and Canadian PIPEDA awareness from day one.
What if I want to cancel?
Cancel anytime before your next monthly cycle. No long-term contract. Your data exports cleanly. We would rather you leave easily than feel locked in.
